## Creating a New Client User

Client account creation requires extensive information due to legal and compliance requirements.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Client Management">
    Go to **Users** > **Clients** in the main navigation, then click **Add Client**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Profile Picture (Optional)">
    Upload a company logo or representative image:
    - Accepted formats: JPG, GIF, or PNG
    - Maximum file size: 500KB
    - Recommended dimensions: 200x200 pixels
    - Click **Upload** or drag and drop the file
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Password">
    Create secure login credentials:
    - **Password**: Minimum 6 characters
    - **Confirm Password**: Must match password
    - Toggle password visibility with the eye icon
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Personal Details">
    Provide company identification:
    
    **Required Fields:**
    - **Name / Business Name**: Legal registered name
    - **Commercial Name**: Trading or brand name
    - **Email**: Primary contact email for login
    - **Phone**: Business contact number with country code
    - **ID Type**: Select business identification type
    - **ID Number**: Business registration number (numbers only)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide Address Information">
    Complete business location details:
    - **Country**: Select business location country
    - **Postal Code**: Business postal/zip code
    - **City**: Business city
    - **Address**: Complete business address
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Legal and Fiscal Documentation">
    Upload all required business documents in PDF format:
    
    <Warning>
      All four documents are **mandatory** and must be uploaded before submission.
    </Warning>
    
    **Required Documents:**
    - **Commercial Registry**: Official business registration certificate
    - **Last Assembly Minutes**: Most recent shareholder meeting minutes
    - **Fiscal Information Registration**: Tax registration certificate
    - **Commercial License**: Business operating license or permit
    
    Each document can be removed and re-uploaded if needed using the trash icon.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Taxpayer Type">
    Specify the client's tax classification:
    - **Taxpayer Type**: Choose between:
      - **Natural Person**: Individual taxpayer
      - **Legal Entity**: Corporate taxpayer
    
    If **Legal Entity** is selected, additional field appears:
    - **Special Condition**: Select either:
      - Special Taxpayer (Contribuyente Especial)
      - Special Withholding Agent (Agente Especial de Retención)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Legal Representative Information">
    Provide details for the authorized company representative:
    - **Name**: Full name of legal representative
    - **ID**: Identification number with type prefix (V, E, etc.)
    - **RIF**: Tax identification number with prefix (J, G, etc.)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Contact Person Information">
    Specify the primary business contact:
    - **Name**: Full name of contact person
    - **ID**: Identification number with type prefix
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Bank Accounts">
    Add at least one bank account for transactions:
    
    **For Each Bank Account:**
    - **Type**: Select bank account country
    - **Bank**: Choose from available banks in selected country
    - **Account Number**: Enter account number
    - **Currency**: Select account currency
    
    **For US Bank Accounts (additional fields):**
    - **SWIFT Code**: International bank identifier
    - **ABA Number**: Routing number (numbers only)
    
    <Tip>
      Click **Add Account** to register multiple bank accounts for different currencies or countries.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Confirm">
    Before final submission:
    - A confirmation dialog will appear
    - Review all entered information
    - Ensure all documents are uploaded
    - Click **Confirm** to create the client account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the Client">
    Click **Save** to create the client account. The system will:
    - Create the client user account
    - Upload all documents to secure storage
    - Send a success notification
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Client Information

To update an existing client's information:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Client">
    - Navigate to the **Client List**
    - Use the search bar to filter by name, email, creation date, or status
    - Click the **eye icon** next to the client to view details
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Edit Mode">
    From the client detail page, click **Edit** to modify information.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Information">
    You can modify:
    - Profile picture/logo
    - Personal and business details
    - Address information
    - Legal documents (replace existing files)
    - Taxpayer information
    - Legal representative details
    - Contact person information
    - Bank accounts (add, edit, or remove)
    
    <Note>
      Email addresses cannot be changed after account creation.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save** to apply changes or **Cancel** to discard them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Legal Documentation Requirements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Commercial Registry (Registro Mercantil)">
    Official document proving the legal registration of the business entity.
    
    **Requirements:**
    - Must be in PDF format
    - Should be current and not expired
    - Must match the business name provided
    - Issued by authorized government entity
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Last Assembly Minutes (Última Acta de Asamblea)">
    Documentation of the most recent shareholder or board meeting.
    
    **Requirements:**
    - Must be in PDF format
    - Should reflect current company structure
    - Must include date of meeting
    - Signed by authorized representatives
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Fiscal Information Registration (Registro de Información Fiscal)">
    Tax registration certificate from the relevant tax authority.
    
    **Requirements:**
    - Must be in PDF format
    - Valid and current registration
    - Includes tax identification number (RIF)
    - Matches taxpayer type selected
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Commercial License (Licencia o Patente Comercial)">
    Business operating license or commercial permit.
    
    **Requirements:**
    - Must be in PDF format
    - Current and not expired
    - Valid for business operations
    - Issued by local authorities
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Taxpayer Type Classification

Understanding the different taxpayer classifications:

### Natural Person (Persona Natural)
Individual taxpayers operating as sole proprietors:
- Individual business owner
- Personal tax identification
- Simplified reporting requirements

### Legal Entity (Persona Jurídica)
Corporate taxpayers with special conditions:

**Special Taxpayer (Contribuyente Especial)**
- Large corporations with special tax obligations
- Enhanced reporting requirements
- May have preferential or special tax treatments

**Special Withholding Agent (Agente Especial de Retención)**
- Authorized to withhold taxes on behalf of tax authority
- Additional compliance responsibilities
- Regular withholding tax reporting

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Document Management">
    - Keep all documents current and up-to-date
    - Use high-quality scans (300 DPI minimum)
    - Ensure documents are legible and complete
    - Store original documents securely offline
    - Review and update annually
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bank Account Setup">
    - Add all relevant bank accounts upfront
    - Verify account numbers before saving
    - Include accounts in multiple currencies for flexibility
    - Update banking information promptly when changed
    - Test small transactions to verify account details
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Contact Information">
    - Ensure email addresses are monitored regularly
    - Provide direct phone numbers for urgent matters
    - Update contact persons when roles change
    - Verify legal representative information is current
    - Maintain backup contact information
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Cannot Submit Form">
    **Issue**: Save button disabled or submission fails
    
    **Common Causes**:
    - Missing required documents
    - Invalid email format
    - Passwords don't match
    - No bank accounts added
    - Missing legal representative or contact information
    
    **Solution**: Review the confirmation dialog for specific missing fields.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Document Upload Errors">
    **Issue**: Cannot upload legal documents
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Ensure files are in PDF format only
    - Check file size is reasonable (under 10MB recommended)
    - Verify internet connection is stable
    - Try uploading documents one at a time
    - Clear browser cache and retry
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Bank Account Issues">
    **Issue**: Cannot add or validate bank accounts
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Verify account number format is correct
    - For US accounts, ensure ABA contains only numbers
    - Check that selected bank matches the country
    - Ensure currency is valid for selected country
    - Verify SWIFT code format is correct
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
